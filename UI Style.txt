Project Brief: CalThai AI

1. Product Vision

CalThai AI is a modern, intuitive, and high-empathy calorie tracker specifically optimized for Thai cuisine. By leveraging AI-powered visual recognition, it removes the friction of manual logging, helping users manage their nutrition with accuracy and ease.

2. Core Value Proposition





Frictionless Tracking: Instant identification of complex Thai street food and home-cooked meals via camera.



Cultural Specialization: Deep accuracy for Thai dishes, including specific macro and sodium insights.



Non-Judgmental Guidance: A friendly, health-focused aesthetic that encourages consistency without guilt.

3. Target Audience





Health-conscious individuals in Thailand or fans of Thai cuisine.



Users looking for a faster alternative to traditional manual food logging apps.



People managing specific dietary goals (weight loss, muscle gain, sodium restriction).

4. Visual Identity & Design System





Style: Modern Minimalist, Health & Fitness aesthetic.



Tone: Warm, friendly, professional, and high-empathy.



Color Palette:





Primary: Fresh Mint Green (#10B981) - representing health and growth.



Accent: Light Coral/Orange - used for calories and highlights.



Background: Clean White (#FFFFFF) and Soft Neutral Grays (#F8F9FF) for a spacious, clean feel.



Typography: Clean sans-serif (Inter) with a clear hierarchy for readability.



Components: Rounded corners (8px), subtle elevation, and pulsing AI indicators.

5. Feature Specifications

A. Daily Dashboard





Circular Progress Ring: Real-time visualization of "Kcal Left" vs. daily goal.



Macro Breakdown: At-a-glance cards for Protein, Carbs, and Fat progress.



Daily Log: Chronological timeline of meals (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks) with food thumbnails.



Smart Navigation: Persistent bottom navigation for quick access to history, scanning, and profile.

B. AI Food Scanner (Hero Feature)





Real-time Viewfinder: Full-screen camera interface with AI bounding boxes.



Motion-Driven Feedback: Animated horizontal scanning line and pulsing glow effects to indicate active "Analyzing..." state.



Daily Scan Quota: "AI Scans Left Today" badge to manage API usage and encourage premium conversion.



Post-Scan Modal: Instant match results with 98% accuracy, serving size selection (Small/Regular/Large), and macro summary.

C. Premium Subscription (Paywall)





Value Triggers: Unlimited AI scans, detailed sodium insights, and Health app synchronization.



Pricing Strategy: Focus on a "Best Value" Yearly Plan (฿890/year) vs. Monthly Plan (฿99/month).



Onboarding: High-converting "Start 7-Day Free Trial" call-to-action.

6. Success Metrics





Daily Active Users (DAU): Monitoring engagement with the calorie ring.



Scan Conversion Rate: Percentage of scans that result in a logged meal.



Premium Conversion: Percentage of users upgrading from the 10-scan daily limit.



Retention: Average meal logging streak per user.
